# Business model

1. Product is free - Make money in the backend (YouTube)
2. Freemium model - Pay to upgrade (Linkedin)
3. Price based on average value to customer - (Hubspot)
4. Price based on product cost - Plus margin (McDonald's)
5. Price with recurring low subscription payments (Netflix) - 150 million users
6. Tiered pricing based on volume (More you do less the price)
7. Revenue as a percentage of every transaction (Commision)
8. Low product price but support is extra 
9. Low entry price, with priced features additional (Cars)
10. Low price base but making money on disposable (Razor)
