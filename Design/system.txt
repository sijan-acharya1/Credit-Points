# What is Credit Points?

Credit Points is a unifying reward points platform where users can buy, sell, trade reward points which they accumulate from different organisations.
Reward points can be gained from purchasing items from eligible vendors.

# Functional requirements
- Create an account for the user
- Send Credit Points from one account to another account
- Buy Credit Points
- Sell Credit Points
- Exchange Credit Points and other reward points
- View account balance
- Give analytics for account balance
- Detailed data on transfer details, spending patterns and behaviour
